# Lorenzo Data Platform

## Context

Use this story when asked about data platforms, metrics, incentive systems, executive reporting, product analytics, or how to turn user activity into roadmap decisions.

## Core facts

- Company: Lorenzo Protocol.
- Role: Lead Data Product Analyst.
- Period: July 2024 to February 2025.
- Product context: fintech data product environment focused on transaction-flow analytics, product metrics, incentive performance, and executive decision support.
- Partnered with Product, Engineering, and leadership to translate user activity, transaction or asset-flow data, and reward performance into product decisions.
- Built dashboards tracking active users, participation, duration, asset flows, conversion patterns, reward-structure performance, and retention or operational review signals.
- Supported fixed versus tiered reward design, engagement review, and product iteration.

## Interview value

This story proves data-product depth beyond compliance. It shows how to build the measurement layer for a financial product, connect metrics to incentives, and support product tradeoffs with a shared data foundation.

## Reusable answer points

- Start with the product question: which behaviors show healthy engagement, retention, and reward effectiveness?
- Explain the data platform work: define the metrics, connect activity and transaction-flow signals, build dashboards, and make tradeoffs visible.
- Show collaboration: Product, Engineering, and leadership used the data to review incentive design and iteration decisions.
- Connect to payments: payment products also need reliable telemetry across conversion, transaction success, exceptions, user behavior, and operational health.
