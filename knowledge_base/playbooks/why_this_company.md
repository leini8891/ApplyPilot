# Why This Company

## Context

Use this playbook to answer "Why us?" for payments, financial infrastructure, compliance, risk, or data-platform companies.

## Core facts

- The company should be connected to one or more of these themes: payments infrastructure, merchant/customer experience, regulated operations, risk controls, platform scalability, or data-driven product decision-making.
- For Adyen specifically, emphasize merchant experience, global payments infrastructure, unified commerce, reliability, and practical product ownership.
- The answer should be specific to the company, but reuse the same career bridge.

## Interview value

This keeps the answer from becoming generic admiration. It connects the company to a concrete product surface where the candidate has relevant experience.

## Reusable answer points

- "I am attracted to companies where product work sits close to money movement, customer trust, and operational reliability."
- "Your product problems match the work I have enjoyed most: converting complex payment, risk, data, and workflow constraints into clear product decisions."
- "For Adyen, the merchant experience angle is especially interesting because reliability, authorization outcomes, operational visibility, and support recovery directly affect business results."
- "I can bring a fresh adjacent perspective from payment/order systems, KYC/AML dashboards, transaction analytics, and workflow automation."
